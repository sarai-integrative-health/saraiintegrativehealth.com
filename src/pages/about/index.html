About Sumandeep Sarai Groh MD

Dr. Groh received a University Curators' Scholarship to attend the University of Missouri Kansas City where she was a member of the Phi Kappa Phi Honor Society. After receiving her MD from the University of Missouri Kansas City, she completed her residency in pediatrics at Children's Mercy Hospital in Kansas City, Missouri.

She is a diplomate of the National Board of Medical Examiners and the American Board of Pediatrics.

Prior to moving to the Asheville area, she was a staff physician at Cook Fort Worth Children's Hospital in Fort Worth, Texas as well as a clinical instructor at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor and an assistant professor at the University of Virginia, Charlottesville.

In 2016 Dr Groh completed her fellowship in Integrative Medicine with Dr Andrew Weil at the University of Arizona and is excited to share her knowledge with patients of all ages.
